I'm a Master's student in Machine Learning at the American University of Sharjah, and I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering with a minor in Engineering Management.

My interests lie in Artificial Intelligence, machine learning, and robotics. Currently, I am focused on research in deploying neural networks on resource-constrained edge devices, with expertise in model compression, quantization techniques, and privacy-preserving methods in edge computing.

Research

My research interests include model compression and optimization for deploying neural networks on embedded systems and resource-constrained edge devices. I focus on quantization techniques, such as integer quantization, mixed-precision quantization, and entropy coding, to achieve efficient and compact models. Additionally, I explore privacy-preserving methods in edge computing, energy-efficient computing, and intelligent autonomous robotics.
